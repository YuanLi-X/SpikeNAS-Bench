
This folder holds the results of training under the CIFAR10, CIFAR100, TinyImagenet dataset.

There are three files in this folder: CIFAR10, CIFAR100, TinyImagenet.

They are the results of networks trained on each of the three datasets.

For example, in the CIFAR100 folder are some numbered folders, different numbers represent different networks, which should range from 0 to 15624, only some of the files are shown here as a demonstration.
